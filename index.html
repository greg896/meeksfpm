About Us

A Cleveland Real Estate Firm You Can Count On

Meeks Residential is a trusted real estate brokerage and property management firm based in Cleveland, Ohio. Since 2009, we have built our reputation on deep market knowledge, personalized client attention, and end-to-end service that covers every aspect of real estate.
