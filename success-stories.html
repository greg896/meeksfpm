Mid-Scope Value-Add

The Basement That Became a Bedroom

West Park, Cleveland · Out-of-state owner since 2024

Three-bedroom SFR with an unfinished basement that no tenant had ever used. We added a legal fourth bedroom and second full bath, did a full kitchen renovation, and re-rented as a four-bedroom — into a different (and stronger) tenant pool than the previous three-bed listing. Capitalized value uplift on a 6.5% cap exceeded the renovation cost in year one.

Turnkey Rescue

The $11,300 Lesson on “Turnkey”

East Side, Cleveland · New owner client, 2024

A new client brought us in to clean up a renovation done by a national turnkey vendor. The cosmetic kitchen refresh looked great. The cast-iron drain stack was at end-of-life, the water-heater venting was non-compliant, and the as-is sale had waived all warranties. We remediated, re-tenanted, and stabilized the property — and used the experience to write our own due-diligence checklist (now in the Investor Playbook).
